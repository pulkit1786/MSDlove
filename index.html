Early Days

He was born on july 7th 1981 in Ranchi,Bihar(India) and he loved to play sports from the childhood. He participated in many sports activities at the school level from where his football coach recognize his talent as a wicket-keeper. He was selected in his school cricket team as a wicket keeper then he played some good knocks as a batsman. His coach motivated him to play more cricket as he was aware that he will do something big in the cricket. MS Dhoni worked really hard on his batting and wicket-keeping. He got selected for his state team. It was tough time for him as he was also doing a job and managing both together was getting difficult for him. He took a bold decision by quitting his government job and just to focus on the cricket.

India Team Selection

He debuted in the indian team in the year 2004 against Bangladesh where he got tested again and again. He couldn't perform good in the initial phase but the indian captain sourav ganguly believed in him and backed him again. He scored his first international century against pakistan with the score of 148. He showed his capabilities that day and became the storng contendar to play for a long time. He picked up for the srilanka bilateral series where he scored a 183 in a match, which was stunning knock. He got the captaincy of the indian team for the 2007 T-20 world cup after so many amazing and winning performances. He won the T-20 world cup then became the captain for the india one day team and won 2011 one day cricket world cup. He conquered the champions trophy in the 2013 in england.
